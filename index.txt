r2asciidoc
==========
David Hajage <dhajage@gmail.com>

http://eusebe.github.com/r2asciidoc[r2asciidoc] is a R package for writing document with embeded R commands.

Short example
-------------

----
> x <- matrix(1:4, 2, 2)> x
     [,1] [,2]
[1,]    1    3
[2,]    2    4 
----

----
> ascii(x)
----
|========= 
|1.00|3.00
|2.00|4.00
|=========
